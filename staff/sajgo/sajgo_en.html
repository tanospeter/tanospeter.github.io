Main fields of research
  • Geochemistry, organic geochemistry, including:
    • genesis of crude oil, petroleum source rocks, migration oils, maturity of oils, correlation studies
    • biological marker compounds and thermal history reconstruction based on their reaction kinetics
    • thermal degradation of kerogens and studies on pyrolysis yields
    • geochemistry of organic molecular solutes in geothermal waters and tracing their origin.
  • Other skills
    • pyrolysis gas chromatography of insoluble organic material and other special pyrolysis, simulation techniques
    • More than 30 years experience in geochemistry and analytical chemistry related to organic materials in geosphere (natural and disposal waters, soils, sediments, crude oils, coals, etc.) applying relevant analytical methods (UV/VIS, IR, GC, HPLC, GC/MS, etc) and special separation and enrichment methods.
