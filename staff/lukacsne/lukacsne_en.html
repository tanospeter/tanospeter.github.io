Outstanding results
  • I investigated Cryogenic postglacial transition layer from NW-Namibia in my PhD project, where I used mineralogical and geochemical methods to give paleoenvironmental reconstruction and search for possible impact ejecta, and for classification biomineralization of biomats of chemolithotroph iron-oxidizing bacteria. Since 12 years shock and thermal metamorphism of ordinary chondrites by mineral spectroscopy. Currently I study aqueous alteration of CV3 chondrites and Martian meteorites, and I take part in Úrkút geobiomineralization project. We investigate in collaboration chinese, brazilian (and earlier iranian) Mn- and Mn-Fe ore deposites.
