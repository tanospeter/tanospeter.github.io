Organizational structure of the institute

From January 1st, 2012, the Institute is operating in the Research Centre for Astronomy and Earth Sciences as Institute for Geological and Geochemical Research. The institute has no department structure, instead the laboratories as functional units and three research groups (Environment-Water-Biogeochemistry, Archeometry and Geochemistry & Palaeoclimate R.G.) form the structure of the Institute.
