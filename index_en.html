Welcome to the Homepage of the Institute for Geological and Geochemical Research

From 1 September 2019, the Institute operates within the Research Center for Astronomy and Earth Sciences, under the name of the Institute for Geological and Geochemical Research. The previous class structure has been abolished, currently the laboratories (serving as functional units) and three research groups (Environment-, hydro and biogeochemical RG, Archaeometry RG, Geochemistry and Paleoclimate RG) form the backbone of the Institute.
