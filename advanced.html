Planed advanced features

(age)

Filter entities which have at l east two ages (UTh and/or 14C) between 10000 and 20000BP

Filter entities which have at least one age (UTh and/or 14C) each 1000 years between 10000 and 20000BP

(space)

Filter entities between E20-50 and N10-55

(metadata)

Filter entities: speleothem_type: stalactite, rock_age: Eocene, cover_thickness >1m, drip_type not unknown

Filter entities that are not of rock_age Holocene or unknown

(sample data)

Filter entities that have at least 100 sample data (d13C and/or d18O) between 10000 and 20000BP

Filter entities that have at least 100 sample data (d13C and/or d18O) between 11000 and 17000BP and the age difference between the consecutive sample data < 20 years

(combined)

Filter entities that are actively growing (date_type: Event; actively forming) and lamina counted (corresponding dating lamina table is not empty)

Filter entities between W100-120 and S10-55 and are not composites (speleothem_type: not composites) with at least 3 ages (any kind) back to 12000BP with at least 1000 sample data (d18O)

Filter entities that have at least 100 sample data (d13C) between 10000 and 20000BP, that do not have a hiatus (Event; hiatus)
