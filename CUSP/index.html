Assessment of the robustness of spectral peaks in proxy records with chronological uncertainty


CUSP
Chronological Uncertainty & Spectral Peaks

Developers: István Gábor Hatvani, Péter Tanos and Zoltán Kern

Original publication describing the model: Hatvani, I.G., Tanos, P., Mudelsee, M. and Kern, Z., 2021. Robust periodic signals in proxy records with chronological uncertainty. Quaternary Science Reviews, in review.

Usage

Please input the required for the model to assess if the period sought for can indeed be robustly detected under the given chronological circumstances. Input the chronological uncertainty (Unc [time step]), mean sampling resolution (MSR [sample / time step]) and the period [time step] sought for. Please note that the values can either be in years (yrs) or 1000 years (kyrs), just make sure all the variables have the same measurement unit for time steps. For further details on the underlying model and real-life examples, the user is referred to the original study (Hatvani et al., 2021).
